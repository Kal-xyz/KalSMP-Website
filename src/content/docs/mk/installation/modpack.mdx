Once you have installed a launcher of your choice, you will need to install the modpack so that you can join the server.

- Follow the guide below for the launcher that you are using.

## The Prism Launcher

import { Steps } from '@astrojs/starlight/components';

<Steps>

1. Click on the button to add an instance.

    <ol>
    <img src="../../../src/assets/add-instance.webp"></img>
    </ol>

2. From the windows that opened, select CurseForge from the sidebar.

    <ol>
    <img src="../../../src/assets/select-curseforge.webp"></img>
    </ol>

3. Type **KalSMP** in the search box, select the Modpack, and press the **OK** button at the bottom-right corner.

    <ol>
    <img src="../../../src/assets/select-kalsmp.webp"></img>
    </ol>

4. If a pop-up appears about missing mods, click on the link, download the file and press **OK** to continue.

    <ol>
    <img src="../../../src/assets/blocked-mods.webp"></img>
    <img src="../../../src/assets/mods-found.webp"></img>
    </ol>

5. Finally, press the **Launch** button on the right to launch the game.

    <ol>
    <img src="../../../src/assets/launch-game.webp"></img>
    </ol>

</Steps>

## The CurseForge Launcher

<Steps>

1. Search for **KalSMP** in the search bar at the top and click install.

    <ol>
    <img src="../../../src/assets/search-install.webp"></img>
    </ol>

2. Click the play button to launch the Minecraft Launcher.

    <ol>
    <img src="../../../src/assets/play-kalsmp.webp"></img>
    </ol>

3. Log in with your Microsoft Account.

    <ol>
    <img src="../../../src/assets/login-microsoft.webp"></img>
    </ol>

4. Press the play button and launch the game.

    <ol>
    <img src="../../../src/assets/press-play.webp"></img>
    </ol>
</Steps>

## The ATLauncher

<Steps>

1. Click on the **Packs** tab on the right sidebar.

    <ol>
    <img src="../../../src/assets/packs-tab.webp"></img>
    </ol>

2. Search for **KalSMP** and click the install button.

    <ol>
    <img src="../../../src/assets/install-search.webp"></img>
    </ol>

3. Go to the **Instances** tab on the right and then press play to launch the game.

    <ol>
    <img src="../../../src/assets/play-instance.webp"></img>
    </ol>
</Steps>

## Other Launchers (NOT RECOMMENDED)

If you don't want to use any of the launchers above or you are using the Official Minecraft Launcher, you
will not receive automatic updates and you will have to reinstall all the mods everytime there is an update.

- Click [here](https://mega.nz) to download all the mods as a .zip file.
_(You will have to extract the archive, and copy the mods to your ```.minecraft``` folder)_


**It is still strongly recommended to use the other launchers above!**
